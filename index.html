The 2024 Workshop on AI-empowered Systems Simulation

Kyoto, Japan

November 19th, 2024

Welcome to WASS 2024

The 2024 Workshop on AI-empowered Systems Simulation (WASS 2024) builds upon the growing need for a comprehensive understanding of how artificial intelligence can enhance complex system simulations. With the rapid advancements in AI technology, there is an increasing demand for exploring its integration into various simulation environments, particularly in fields like engineering, healthcare, economics, and more. WASS 2024 aims to bridge the gap between AI research and its practical applications in system simulations, fostering collaboration and knowledge sharing among researchers, developers, and industry experts.
